I Think

Linus Yang

I Think
Wangshu Dai
(1905-1950)

I think, therefore I am a butterfly...
After thousands of years, a tiny flower's whisper
Through a dreamless mist without awakening,
Will shake my bright-coloured wings.

(14 March, 1937)
(Translated by Linus Yang, 11 May, 2011)

我思想
戴望舒
（1905-1950）

我思想，故我是蝴蝶……
万年后小花的轻呼
透过无梦无醒的云雾，
来振撼我斑斓的彩翼。

（1937年3月14日）
